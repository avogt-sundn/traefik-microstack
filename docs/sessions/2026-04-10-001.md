## Hard-won insights

- Axiom IDs are **positional, not permanent** — inserting an axiom above an existing one shifts all IDs below it in that file. Always run `make axioms` to confirm current IDs before referencing them.
- The knowledge map tree in CLAUDE.md doubles as the abbreviation registry. It's the single place to look up what `TRK` or `DCA` means.
